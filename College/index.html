about bluestar University

Founded in 1924, bluestar University has grown from a small liberal college to a comprehensive research university recognized for academic excellence.

Our commitment to student success, innovative teaching methods, and cutting-edge research makes us a leader in higher education.

  • 90+ years of academic excellence
  • Student-faculty ratio of 14:1
  • $120M in annual research funding
